The Bespoke Treatment

This is not a standard facial; it is a comprehensive, results-driven skin treatment designed exclusively for you. By integrating advanced technology with proven clinical actives, we address your unique skin concerns to achieve visible, long-lasting improvements in skin health, texture, and radiance.

  • 1. Consultation & Skin Analysis: We begin with an in-depth consultation and skin analysis to understand your skin history and goals.
  • 2. Tailored Treatment Application: I perform your unique facial, selecting from a range of modalities like microneedling, radio frequency, or Dermalux™ therapy to target your specific needs.
  • 3. Aftercare & Future Planning: We conclude with a detailed aftercare plan and recommendations to ensure you maintain and enhance your results between visits.

Your Questions, Answered

It means your facial is born from conversation and expert analysis, not chosen from a menu. At the start of every session, we discuss your goals and I assess your skin's real-time needs. I then design a unique protocol for you, a truly personal, adaptable approach to skin health.

My first priority is your comfort in my calm, private studio. We will begin with a relaxed, in-depth chat about your skin's history, your lifestyle, and your ultimate goals. I will explain every step of the proposed treatment, ensuring you feel completely informed and at ease. This first session is about building trust and creating a long-term strategy for your skin.

For the vast majority of treatments, there is absolutely no downtime. You will leave looking radiant and can return to your day immediately. For more intensive, corrective modalities like deep microneedling, you may experience a healthy, pink glow for 24-48 hours. We will always discuss this beforehand to ensure your treatment plan fits perfectly with your schedule.

The ideal frequency for facials depends entirely on your skin’s unique needs. As a qualified skin therapist, I assess each client individually to determine the best treatment plan for their skin type, concerns, and goals.
